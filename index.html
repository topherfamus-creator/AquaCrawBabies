Care Guide — quick essentials

Tank Setup

10+ gallon recommended for stable parameters. Plenty of hiding spots (plants, coconut caves). Fine sand or smooth substrate.

Water & Parameters

Temperature: 68–78°F (20–25°C). pH: 6.8–8.2. Gentle filtration — avoid strong currents. Weekly small water changes.

Feeding

Algae wafers, blanched veggies, sinking pellets. Feed lightly — remove uneaten food within 24 hours.

Breeding & Notes

These dwarf crawfish reproduce by parthenogenesis: all individuals produce offspring without mating. Colonies can grow quickly — consider spacing and population control.
